Visit Borama

A peaceful city known for its welcoming people, beautiful green landscapes, refreshing climate, and rich culture that offers visitors both comfort and unforgettable memories.

Top three activities to do at Borama

Explore the green hills and countryside

Borama is one of the greener and cooler cities in Somaliland. The surrounding hills, farms, and valleys give it a calm atmosphere.

Experience the local culture

Visit local markets, Try Somali foods like bariis, camel meat, and fresh shaah. Listen to stories about the city's history and traditions

Visit educational and historical areas

Walk around the university area, Visit older parts of town and community centers
